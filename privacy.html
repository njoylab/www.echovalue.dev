Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 10/12/2023

Introduction

Welcome to echoValue API. We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling any personal information we obtain from you with care and respect. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our services.

Information Collection

echoValue API itself does not collect any personal information from its users. Our service is designed to function without the need for user data collection, ensuring your privacy and security.

Use of Stripe for Payments

For users who choose to access our paid features, we utilize Stripe for payment processing. In these cases, you will be required to provide your email address and payment information directly to Stripe. Please note that Stripe operates independently from echoValue API, and their use of your information is governed by Stripe's own Privacy Policy.

Data Security

We take data security seriously. While echoValue API does not store personal user data, any interaction with our payment processors is protected through industry-standard encryption and security measures.

Changes to This Policy

echoValue API reserves the right to modify this privacy policy at any time. Any changes will be posted on our website and, where appropriate, notified to you by email.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy, please contact us at support at echovalue dot dev.
